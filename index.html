How We Work

From Log to Finished Board

Four controlled stages from raw material selection to export-ready FJLB panels.

STEP 01

Raw Material Selection

Logs are graded by species, moisture content and structural quality. Only approved material enters production.

STEP 02

Cutting & Finger Jointing

Timber is cross-cut, end-profiled with precision finger joints and glued under controlled pressure for maximum bond strength.

STEP 03

Lamination & QC

Finger-jointed strips are edge-glued and cold-pressed into panels. Boards are checked for flatness, thickness tolerance and emission levels.

STEP 04

Sanding & Packaging

Panels are double-belt sanded to specified grit, cut to final dimensions and packaged with full labelling for export or domestic delivery.
